固定搭配-4

说明

  • 💫 释义 ( Meaning )
  • 🫧 例句 ( Example )
  • 🌈 同义 ( Synonym )

Part 31

take responsibility

  • 💫 释义 : 承担责任
    • 🫧 例句 : A good leader takes responsibility for both successes and failures.
    • 🌈 同义 : accept blame, be accountable

take advantage of

  • 💫 释义 : 利用
    • 🫧 例句 : You should take advantage of this opportunity to learn.
    • 🌈 同义 : make use of, capitalize on

take…for granted

  • 💫 释义 : 认为…理所当然
    • 🫧 例句 : We often take our health for granted until we lose it.
    • 🌈 同义 : assume something will always be available

take…into account

  • 💫 释义 : 把…考虑在内
    • 🫧 例句 : When making the decision, we need to take all factors into account.
    • 🌈 同义 : consider, make allowance for

step down

  • 💫 释义 : 辞职; 让位
    • 🫧 例句 : The CEO decided to step down after decades of service.
    • 🌈 同义 : resign, relinquish one’s position

give away

  • 💫 释义 : 赠送; 泄露
    • 🫧 例句 : He decided to give away all his old books to the library.
    • 🌈 同义 : donate, reveal

give back

  • 💫 释义 : 归还; 回报
    • 🫧 例句 : Please give back the money you borrowed last week.
    • 🌈 同义 : return, repay

give in

  • 💫 释义 : 屈服; 让步
    • 🫧 例句 : After a long negotiation, they finally gave in to our demands.
    • 🌈 同义 : surrender, yield

give off

  • 💫 释义 : 发出, 散发出 ( 光、热、气味等 )
    • 🫧 例句 : The flowers give off a sweet fragrance.
    • 🌈 同义 : emit, release

give out

  • 💫 释义 : 分发; 耗尽; 停止运转
    • 🫧 例句 : The teacher gave out the exam papers to the students.
    • 🌈 同义 : distribute, run out

Part 32

give up

  • 💫 释义 : 放弃; 戒除
    • 🫧 例句 : Never give up on your dreams, no matter how hard it gets.
    • 🌈 同义 : quit, abandon

as far back as

  • 💫 释义 : 早在…; 远在… ( 就 )
    • 🫧 例句 : This tradition can be traced as far back as the 18th century.
    • 🌈 同义 : since, going back to

in at least

  • 💫 释义 : 至少在… ( 范围内 )
    • 🫧 例句 : This is the highest temperature in at least five years.
    • 🌈 同义 : within a minimum of, no less than

in the past [number] [time unit]

  • 💫 释义 : 在过去的… ( 时间 ) 里
    • 🫧 例句 : The company has launched three new products in the past six months.
    • 🌈 同义 : during the last, over the preceding

over the last [number] [time unit]

  • 💫 释义 : 在过去的… ( 时间 ) 里
    • 🫧 例句 : Sales have increased by 20% over the last quarter.
    • 🌈 同义 : during the past, in the recent

within a [time unit] span

  • 💫 释义 : 在…的时间跨度内
    • 🫧 例句 : She was promoted twice within a one-year span.
    • 🌈 同义 : during a period of, in a window of

year-over-year ( YOY )

  • 💫 释义 : 同比的 ( 与去年同期相比 )
    • 🫧 例句 : The company reported a 10% year-over-year growth in revenue.
    • 🌈 同义 : compared to the same period last year

quarter-over-quarter ( QOQ )

  • 💫 释义 : 环比的 ( 与上一季度相比 )
    • 🫧 例句 : Profits fell 2% quarter-over-quarter.
    • 🌈 同义 : compared to the previous quarter

bear the brunt of

  • 💫 释义 : 承受…的主要冲击
    • 🫧 例句 : Small businesses are bearing the brunt of the economic downturn.
    • 🌈 同义 : suffer the worst part of

take a hit

  • 💫 释义 : 遭受打击, 蒙受损失
    • 🫧 例句 : Our investments took a hit during the market crash.
    • 🌈 同义 : suffer a loss, be negatively affected

Part 33

hedge against ( inflation/risk )

  • 💫 释义 : 对冲 ( 通胀/风险 )
    • 🫧 例句 : Many investors buy gold to hedge against inflation.
    • 🌈 同义 : protect oneself from

tide over

  • 💫 释义 : 帮助渡过 ( 难关 )
    • 🫧 例句 : The loan was enough to tide the company over the cash flow crisis.
    • 🌈 同义 : help through a difficult period

tighten one’s belt

  • 💫 释义 : 勒紧裤腰带, 省吃俭用
    • 🫧 例句 : After the job loss, the family had to tighten their belts.
    • 🌈 同义 : spend less, economize

risk appetite

  • 💫 释义 : 风险偏好
    • 🫧 例句 : Younger investors often have a higher risk appetite.
    • 🌈 同义 : tolerance for risk

blue-chip stocks

  • 💫 释义 : 蓝筹股 ( 信誉良好、经营稳健的公司股票 )
    • 🫧 例句 : His portfolio is mainly composed of blue-chip stocks.
    • 🌈 同义 : high-quality stocks, reliable performers

bottom out

  • 💫 释义 : 触底, 降至最低点后趋于稳定
    • 🫧 例句 : The housing market is finally showing signs of bottoming out.
    • 🌈 同义 : reach the lowest point

is up from

  • 💫 释义 : 相较于…有所上升/增加
    • 🫧 例句 : The new offer is up from the initial proposal.
    • 🌈 同义 : has increased from, is higher than

is down from

  • 💫 释义 : 相较于…有所下降/减少
    • 🫧 例句 : This quarter’s revenue is down from the same period last year.
    • 🌈 同义 : has decreased from, is lower than

shows an increase over

  • 💫 释义 : 与…相比显示出增长
    • 🫧 例句 : The data shows a sharp increase over the previous month.
    • 🌈 同义 : demonstrates growth compared to

represents a jump from

  • 💫 释义 : 表示从…跃升
    • 🫧 例句 : The latest figure represents a significant jump from earlier estimates.
    • 🌈 同义 : constitutes a rise from

Part 34

has climbed since

  • 💫 释义 : 自…以来已攀升
    • 🫧 例句 : Public confidence has climbed since the new policy was implemented.
    • 🌈 同义 : has risen since

fewer than

  • 💫 释义 : 少于 ( 用于可数名词 )
    • 🫧 例句 : Fewer than ten students attended the lecture.
    • 🌈 同义 : less than ( 用于可数名词 )

go off

  • 💫 释义 : ( 炸弹 ) 爆炸; ( 警报 ) 响起; 突然发出巨响
    • 🫧 例句 : The fire alarm went off in the middle of the night.
    • 🌈 同义 : explode, sound loudly
  • 💫 释义 : ( 食物 ) 变质, 坏掉
    • 🫧 例句 : The milk has gone off; don’t drink it.
    • 🌈 同义 : spoil, go bad
  • 💫 释义 : 离开 ( 去做某事 ); 进展
    • 🫧 例句 : She went off to start her own business. / The event went off smoothly.
    • 🌈 同义 : depart, proceed

send out

  • 💫 释义 : 发出, 发送 ( 信号、邀请、邮件等 )
    • 🫧 例句 : The company sent out a press release to all major media.
    • 🌈 同义 : dispatch, issue, distribute
  • 💫 释义 : 派遣 ( 人员 )
    • 🫧 例句 : The UN decided to send out a peacekeeping force.
    • 🌈 同义 : deploy, dispatch

send out for

  • 💫 释义 : 打电话订购 ( 外卖、货物 )
    • 🫧 例句 : Let’s send out for pizza tonight.
    • 🌈 同义 : order in, have delivered

other than

  • 💫 释义 : 除了; 不同于
    • 🫧 例句 : There’s no one here other than me.
    • 🌈 同义 : except, apart from
  • 💫 释义 : 除了…之外 ( 用于否定句 )
    • 🫧 例句 : She has no income other than her salary.
    • 🌈 同义 : besides, aside from

to begin with

  • 💫 释义 : 首先, 第一 ( 用于列举理由或要点 )
    • 🫧 例句 : To begin with, we need to clarify the project’s main objective.
    • 🌈 同义 : firstly, in the first place
  • 💫 释义 : 起初, 一开始
    • 🫧 例句 : There were only a few members to begin with.
    • 🌈 同义 : at the start, initially

match with

  • 💫 释义 : 与…相配/相适合
    • 🫧 例句 : Your tie matches perfectly with your shirt.
    • 🌈 同义 : go well with, complement
  • 💫 释义 : 为…找到相配的人或物
    • 🫧 例句 : The program matches volunteers with local schools.
    • 🌈 同义 : pair with, connect

be matched with

  • 💫 释义 : 被安排与…对应/比较
    • 🫧 例句 : In the study, participants were matched with a control group.
    • 🌈 同义 : be paired with, be aligned with

tipping point

  • 💫 释义 : 临界点, 转折点 ( 指引发重大、不可逆变化的微小积累的最终节点 )
    • 🫧 例句 : The scandal was the tipping point that led to the CEO’s resignation.
    • 🌈 同义 : turning point, breaking point, critical juncture

Part 35

all life on Earth

  • 💫 释义 : 地球上的一切生命
    • 🫧 例句 : Climate change is a threat to all life on Earth.
    • 🌈 同义 : every living thing on the planet, the entirety of Earth’s biosphere
  • 💫 释义 : 地球众生 ( 常用于生态、哲学语境 )
    • 🫧 例句 : This discovery could change how we understand all life on Earth.
    • 🌈 同义 : the whole of Earth’s life, the entire biological world

such…that…

  • 💫 释义 : 如此…以至于… ( 引导结果状语从句 )
    • 🫧 例句 : It was such a good film that I watched it twice.
    • 🌈 同义 : so…that… ( 修饰形容词或副词 )
  • 💫 释义 : 这样…的 ( 事物 ) 以至于…
    • 🫧 例句 : He spoke with such passion that the audience was captivated.
    • 🌈 同义 : to the extent that
  • 💫 释义 : 如此大的…以至于…
    • 🫧 例句 : There was such a long queue that we decided to come back later.
    • 🌈 同义 : a very…so that

slip from

  • 💫 释义 : 从…滑落; 从…脱离
    • 🫧 例句 : The glass slipped from her hand and shattered on the floor.
    • 🌈 同义 : fall from, drop from
  • 💫 释义 : ( 从记忆、意识中 ) 消失, 被遗忘
    • 🫧 例句 : The details of the event have slipped from my memory.
    • 🌈 同义 : fade from, escape from

slip from view

  • 💫 释义 : 从视线中消失
    • 🫧 例句 : The boat slipped from view behind the fog.
    • 🌈 同义 : disappear from sight

not a moment too soon

  • 💫 释义 : 正是时候, 毫不嫌早 ( 强调某事早该发生 )
    • 🫧 例句 : The ambulance arrived, not a moment too soon.
    • 🌈 同义 : just in time, none too soon
  • 💫 释义 : 来得刚好 ( 带庆幸语气 )
    • 🫧 例句 : The rain started just as we got home— not a moment too soon !
    • 🌈 同义 : in the nick of time
  • 💫 释义 : 早该如此 ( 对迟来事物的评价 )
    • 🫧 例句 : This policy reform comes not a moment too soon for the struggling industry.
    • 🌈 同义 : long overdue, highly timely

for sb to do sth

  • 💫 释义 : 由某人做某事 ( 作主语、表语、宾语等 )
    • 🫧 例句 : For him to admit a mistake is very difficult.
    • 🌈 同义 : that sb should do sth
  • 💫 释义 : 让某人做某事 ( 与形容词连用 )
    • 🫧 例句 : This book is too difficult for a child to understand.
    • 🌈 同义 : ( used after adjectives )
  • 💫 释义 : 供某人做某事 ( 作定语 )
    • 🫧 例句 : There’s a lot of work for us to finish today.
    • 🌈 同义 : that sb needs to do

responded to… by…

  • 💫 释义 : 通过…方式来应对/回应…
    • 🫧 例句 : The company responded to the crisis by implementing new safety protocols.
    • 🌈 同义 : reacted to… by…, answered… by…
  • 💫 释义 : 以…行动作为对…的回应
    • 🫧 例句 : She responded to the challenge by working even harder.
    • 🌈 同义 : met… with the action of…
  • 💫 释义 : 针对…采取了…行动
    • 🫧 例句 : The government responded to public pressure by launching an investigation.
    • 🌈 同义 : addressed… through the means of…

turn…green

  • 💫 释义 : 使…变绿 ( 指绿化 )
    • 🫧 例句 : The community project aims to turn the vacant lot green.
    • 🌈 同义 : make…green, greenify
  • 💫 释义 : 使…变得环保
    • 🫧 例句 : New technology can help turn the industry green.
    • 🌈 同义 : make…environmentally friendly

turn green

  • 💫 释义 : 变绿; 脸色变得苍白 ( 带绿色, 指身体不适 )
    • 🫧 例句 : The leaves turn green in spring. / His face turned green after the roller coaster ride.
    • 🌈 同义 : become green, look pale and sickly

war on

  • 💫 释义 : 对…的战争, 打击…的行动
    • 🫧 例句 : The government declared a war on drugs.
    • 🌈 同义 : campaign against, crackdown on
  • 💫 释义 : 针对…的大规模斗争
    • 🫧 例句 : The organization is committed to the war on poverty.
    • 🌈 同义 : battle against, fight against
  • 💫 释义 : 消除…的运动
    • 🫧 例句 : The city launched a war on graffiti.
    • 🌈 同义 : offensive against, assault on

Part 36

apart from

  • 💫 释义 : 除了…之外 ( 都 )
    • 🫧 例句 : Apart from a few scratches, the car is in good condition.
    • 🌈 同义 : except for, other than
  • 💫 释义 : 除了…之外 ( 还 )
    • 🫧 例句 : Apart from English, he speaks French and Japanese.
    • 🌈 同义 : in addition to, besides
  • 💫 释义 : 脱离, 远离
    • 🫧 例句 : He stood apart from the crowd, watching silently.
    • 🌈 同义 : separate from, away from

defence against

  • 💫 释义 : 对…的防御; 针对…的防护
    • 🫧 例句 : Vaccination is the best defence against the virus.
    • 🌈 同义 : protection from, safeguard against
  • 💫 释义 : 抵御…的手段
    • 🫧 例句 : A strong immune system is your body’s defence against infection.
    • 🌈 同义 : resistance to, barrier against
  • 💫 释义 : 为…辩护
    • 🫧 例句 : The lawyer’s defence against the accusations was very convincing.
    • 🌈 同义 : justification for, argument in response to

different from

  • 💫 释义 : 与…不同
    • 🫧 例句 : His approach is different from mine.
    • 🌈 同义 : distinct from, unlike
  • 💫 释义 : 区别于…
    • 🫧 例句 : The final product was different from the original design.
    • 🌈 同义 : not the same as
  • 💫 释义 : 与…有差异
    • 🫧 例句 : Life in the country is very different from life in the city.
    • 🌈 同义 : dissimilar to

have…in common with…

  • 💫 释义 : 与…有共同之处
    • 🫧 例句 : The two cultures have many traditions in common with each other.
    • 🌈 同义 : share…with…, be similar to…in…
  • 💫 释义 : 与…共享…
    • 🫧 例句 : I have nothing in common with my roommate.
    • 🌈 同义 : possess mutual…with…
  • 💫 释义 : 与…在…上相似
    • 🫧 例句 : This theory has some key points in common with earlier research.
    • 🌈 同义 : resemble…in aspects of…

in response to

  • 💫 释义 : 作为对…的回应
    • 🫧 例句 : The company lowered its prices in response to customer feedback.
    • 🌈 同义 : reacting to, as a reply to
  • 💫 释义 : 针对…而采取的行动
    • 🫧 例句 : In response to the crisis, the government issued new guidelines.
    • 🌈 同义 : answering, addressing
  • 💫 释义 : 应…而作出
    • 🫧 例句 : This program was created in response to market demands.
    • 🌈 同义 : as a result of, due to

roll out

  • 💫 释义 : 正式推出 ( 新产品、服务 )
    • 🫧 例句 : The company will roll out the new software next month.
    • 🌈 同义 : launch, introduce
  • 💫 释义 : 铺开, 实施 ( 计划、政策 )
    • 🫧 例句 : The new policy will be rolled out nationwide.
    • 🌈 同义 : implement, deploy
  • 💫 释义 : 展开, 铺开 ( 物理物件 )
    • 🫧 例句 : They rolled out the red carpet for the important guests.
    • 🌈 同义 : unfold, spread out

meet expectations

  • 💫 释义 : 达到期望, 符合预期
    • 🫧 例句 : The new product met expectations and sold well.
    • 🌈 同义 : fulfill expectations, satisfy expectations
  • 💫 释义 : 如愿以偿
    • 🫧 例句 : The film’s plot was predictable and failed to meet expectations.
    • 🌈 同义 : live up to expectations
  • 💫 释义 : 不负众望
    • 🫧 例句 : The team worked hard to meet expectations.
    • 🌈 同义 : come up to expectations

get off

  • 💫 释义 : 下车; 离开
    • 🫧 例句 : We need to get off at the next bus stop.
    • 🌈 同义 : alight, disembark
  • 💫 释义 : 脱下 ( 衣物 )
    • 🫧 例句 : Get your wet shoes off before you come in.
    • 🌈 同义 : take off, remove
  • 💫 释义 : 下班; 结束工作
    • 🫧 例句 : I usually get off work at 6 p.m.
    • 🌈 同义 : finish work, knock off
  • 💫 释义 : ( 使 ) 免受惩罚
    • 🫧 例句 : He was surprised to get off with just a warning.
    • 🌈 同义 : escape punishment, be let off

get off the ground

  • 💫 释义 : 顺利开始, 取得初步进展 ( 指项目、计划等 )
    • 🫧 例句 : Without more funding, the project will never get off the ground.
    • 🌈 同义 : become established, make a start
  • 💫 释义 : 启动, 付诸实施
    • 🫧 例句 : The new initiative has finally gotten off the ground.
    • 🌈 同义 : launch successfully, become operational
  • 💫 释义 : 起步, 起飞
    • 🫧 例句 : It took several months for the business to get off the ground.
    • 🌈 同义 : begin to succeed, gain momentum

make up

  • 💫 释义 : 组成, 构成
    • 🫧 例句 : These five companies make up the core of the industry.
    • 🌈 同义 : constitute, form
  • 💫 释义 : 编造, 虚构
    • 🫧 例句 : He had to make up an excuse for being late.
    • 🌈 同义 : invent, fabricate
  • 💫 释义 : 补足, 弥补
    • 🫧 例句 : I need to make up the hours I missed yesterday.
    • 🌈 同义 : compensate for, recover
  • 💫 释义 : 化妆
    • 🫧 例句 : She took only five minutes to make up her face.
    • 🌈 同义 : apply cosmetics
  • 💫 释义 : 和解, 和好
    • 🫧 例句 : They argued but later made up.
    • 🌈 同义 : reconcile, settle a dispute

Part 37

build reputation on…

  • 💫 释义 : 凭借…建立声誉
    • 🫧 例句 : The company built its reputation on reliable products.
