断点1

💫： 与...相关

💫： 与...有关；对...有影响
🫧： Your personal health has a direct bearing on your work performance.

💫： 偶尔；有时
🫧： He visits the city on occasion.

on multiple occasions
💫： 多次；数次
🫧： I've reminded him on multiple occasions.

have the occasion to do sth
💫： 有机会做某事
🫧： I hope you have the occasion to visit our new headquarters.

💫： 使被理解
🫧： It's a difficult idea to get across.

💫： 使某人铭记
🫧： I must impress upon you the need for confidentiality.

drive home
💫： 深刻认识
🫧： He used a real-life example to drive home his point.

draw...from...
💫： 吸引；引出
🫧： The speaker drew applause from the audience. / The investigation drew criticism from the public.

treat...as...
💫： 将...视为...
🫧： The manager always treats her employees as equals, which fosters a positive work environment.
🌈： regard...as..., see...as..., view...as..., deem...(to be)..., look upon...as..., think of...as..., label...as..., perceive...as...

💫： 结果是/生产/出席
🫧： It turned out to be a great success.

turn up
💫： 调高/出现
🫧： Can you turn up the volume? / My keys finally turned up.

turn down
💫： 调低/拒绝
🫧： Please turn down the music. / She turned down the job offer.

💫： 上交/睡觉

💫： 触发，启动 ( 警报、装置 )

🌈： activate, trigger

set off
💫： 引发，导致 ( 一系列事件 )
🫧： His resignation set off a chain of management changes.
🌈： spark, cause to begin

set off
💫： 出发，启程
🫧： They set off on their journey at dawn.
🌈： begin a journey, start out

set off
💫： 衬托，使显眼
🫧： The blue frame sets off the painting perfectly.
🌈： enhance, make more noticeable

set out
💫： 出发/陈述/打算
🫧： They set out on a journey. / He set out his plans clearly.

set about
💫： 开始着手
🫧： She set about cleaning the room.

set aside
💫： 留出/搁置/驳回
🫧： Set aside some money each month. / Let's set aside our differences.

set back
💫： 阻碍/耽误
🫧： The project was set back by bad weather.

💫： 开始并持续 ( 指不好的事 )
🫧： Winter has set in.

set apart
💫： 使与众不同/留出
🫧： Her talent sets her apart.

set on / upon
💫： 袭击，攻击
🫧： He was set upon by thieves.

set against
💫： 使反对/以...为背景
🫧： The scandal set the public against him. / The city is beautiful set against the mountains.

set forth
💫： 陈述，阐明
🫧： The document sets forth the terms of the agreement.

guideline on
💫： 关于...的指南
🫧： Please follow the guideline on data security.

guideline for
💫： 为...制定的指南
🫧： Here is a guideline for new employees.

guideline regarding
💫： 有关...的指导方针
🫧： The government issued a guideline regarding tax filing.

up to it
💫： 有能力、有精力或状态佳以应对某种挑战或完成某项任务
🫧： I know the project is tough, but I'm sure you're up to it.
🌈： capable of (doing) something, fit for something, in fit state, Equal to the task / challenge, have what it takes, in the right state of mind (or body)

engage in (sth/doing sth)
💫： 参与，从事 ( 某项活动或工作 )
🫧： It's not a topic I would engage in debate about.
🌈： participate in, take part in, be involved in

engage with sb/sth
💫： 与...建立联系；吸引；致力于理解或处理
🫧： The company uses social media to engage with its customers.
🌈： connect with, interact with, involve oneself with

engage sb in sth
💫： 使某人参与/卷入某事
🫧： The teacher tried to engage all the students in the class discussion.
🌈： involve sb in, draw sb into, include sb in

engage sb's interest / attention
💫： 吸引某人的兴趣/注意力
🫧： The first chapter of the book is designed to engage the reader's interest immediately.
🌈： capture/grab one's interest/attention, hold one's attention, fascinate

engage sb to do sth
💫： 雇佣/聘请某人做某事
🫧： We will engage a professional designer to create the logo.
🌈： hire/employ sb to do sth, appoint sb to do sth

engage (as sth)
💫： ( 以...身份 ) 聘用
🫧： She was engaged as a consultant on the project.
🌈： hired as, employed as, appointed as

be engaged to sb
💫： 与某人订婚
🫧： I'm happy to announce that my daughter is engaged to a wonderful man.
🌈： be betrothed to (非常正式), have pledged to marry

be engaged in sth
💫： 正忙于，正从事于 ( 状态 )
🫧： He was engaged in a deep conversation and didn't notice me.
🌈： be busy with, be occupied with, be absorbed in

be forced into (sth/doing sth)
💫： 被迫 ( 接受某种状态或做某事 )
🫧： Many small businesses were forced into bankruptcy during the economic crisis.
🌈： be compelled to do sth, be coerced into, be pressured into, be left with no choice but to do

come across sb/sth
💫： 偶然遇见，碰见
🫧： I came across this old photo while cleaning the attic.
🌈： run into, stumble upon, chance upon, find by chance

come across (as sth)
💫： 给人留下...印象；显得
🫧： He comes across as very confident in interviews.
🌈： seem, appear, strike someone as, give the impression of being

come across (with sth)
💫： 提供 ( 所需之物，如钱、信息 ) ；交出
🫧： He finally came across with the money he owed me.
🌈： provide, deliver, hand over, come up with

come across (sth)
💫： ( 想法、信息等 ) 被理解，被传达
🫧： His main point didn't come across clearly in the speech.
🌈： be understood, be communicated, get across, be conveyed

above all else
💫： 尤其是；最重要的是
🫧： Above all else, we must ensure the safety of our staff.
🌈： most importantly, first and foremost, primarily

above all
💫： 尤其是；最重要的是
🫧： He is a great leader, and above all, a good person.
🌈： most of all, more than anything else, chiefly

first and foremost
💫： 首先；首要的是
🫧： We are, first and foremost, a technology company.
🌈： primarily, principally, in the first place

most importantly
💫： 最重要的是
🫧： We need to finish the project on time and, most importantly, within budget.
🌈： crucially, critically, above all

favour A over B
💫： 偏爱A胜过B；更倾向于选择A而非B
🫧： Many investors currently favour bonds over stocks for stability.
🌈： prefer A to B, choose A over B, value A more than B

prefer A to B
💫： 比起B来更喜欢A
🫧： I prefer tea to coffee in the morning.
🌈： favour A over B, like A better than B, would rather A than B

would rather A than B
💫： 宁愿A也不愿B
🫧： I would rather stay home than go to a crowded party.
🌈： prefer to A rather than B, would sooner A than B

opt for A instead of B
💫： 选择A而不是B
🫧： She opted for the vegetarian menu instead of the fish dish.
🌈： choose A over B, go for A rather than B, select A in place of B

give precedence to A over B
💫： 给予A优先权胜过B ( 正式 )
🫧： The committee gave precedence to environmental concerns over economic gains.
🌈： prioritize A above B, rank A higher than B

not just... but also...
💫： 不仅...而且...
🫧： The new policy is not just inefficient, but also costly.
🌈： not only... but also..., not merely... but...

not only... but also...
💫： 不仅...而且... ( 比 not just 稍正式 )
🫧： She is not only a talented writer but also an accomplished painter.
🌈： not just... but also..., not merely... but..., as well as

not merely... but...
💫： 不仅仅...而是... ( 强调后者更重要 )
🫧： This is not merely a setback, but a critical failure.
🌈： not just... but..., not only... but..., not simply... but...

...as well as...
💫： ...和...；既...又... ( 强调前者 )
🫧： The system is efficient as well as user-friendly.
🌈： and also, in addition to, along with

both... and...
💫： 两者都...；既...又... ( 强调并列 )
🫧： The project requires both time and money.
🌈： and, as well as

over and above
💫： 除...之外 ( 还 ) ( 正式 )
🫧： He received a bonus over and above his regular salary.
🌈： in addition to, besides, on top of

bring in sth
💫： 赚取，产生 ( 收入 )
🫧： The new product line is expected to bring in significant revenue.
🌈： earn, generate, yield, produce

bring in sb
💫： 邀请…参与；引入 ( 专家、外部力量 )
🫧： We need to bring in a consultant to solve this technical issue.
🌈： call in, enlist, recruit, hire

bring in sth
💫： 推行，实施 ( 新法律、规则或系统 )
🫧： The government plans to bring in new regulations next year.
🌈： introduce, implement, institute, enact

bring in sb
💫： 逮捕并带至警局
🫧： The police brought in two suspects for questioning.
🌈： arrest, take into custody, apprehend

in light of
💫： 考虑到，鉴于
🫧： The meeting was rescheduled in light of the manager's sudden illness.
🌈： considering, taking into account, given, in view of

in view of
💫： 鉴于，考虑到
🫧： In view of recent developments, we need to change our strategy.
🌈： considering, in light of, taking into account, given

taking into account
💫： 考虑到，把...纳入考虑
🫧： Taking into account all the factors, this is our best option.
🌈： considering, bearing in mind, in view of, allowing for

given that
💫： 考虑到，鉴于 ( 后接从句 )
🫧： Given that the market is volatile, we should be cautious.
🌈： considering that, in view of the fact that, seeing that

on account of
💫： 由于，因为
🫧： The event was postponed on account of bad weather.
🌈： because of, due to, owing to, by reason of

owing to
💫： 由于，因为
🫧： Owing to unforeseen circumstances, the project has been delayed.
🌈： due to, because of, on account of, as a result of

in terms of
💫： 在...方面；从...角度来说
🫧： The project is successful in terms of its final outcome.
🌈： with regard to, regarding, from the perspective of, as regards, in the aspect of

come to terms with
💫： 接受 ( 困难或不愉快的现实 ) ；与...达成妥协
🫧： It took him years to come to terms with his loss.
🌈： accept, reconcile oneself to, learn to live with, make peace with

in the long/short term
💫： 从长/短期来看
🫧： This decision will benefit the company in the long term.
🌈： over the long/short haul, from a long/short-term perspective

on good/bad terms (with)
💫： ( 与某人 ) 关系好/不好
🫧： She is on good terms with all her colleagues.
🌈： have a good/bad relationship with, be friendly with/be at odds with

a term of art
💫： 专业术语；特定领域内的专门用语
🫧： "Liquidated damages" is a term of art in contract law.
🌈： technical term, specialized terminology, jargon

be on speaking terms
💫： 关系尚可 ( 至少能交谈 )
🫧： They are not friends, but they are on speaking terms.
🌈： be civil to each other, maintain cordial relations

argument for

🫧： One strong argument for remote work is increased productivity.
🌈： case for, reason for, justification for

make a case for
💫： 提出理由支持
🫧： The report makes a compelling case for increased funding.
🌈： argue for, advocate for, present arguments for

in favor of

🌈： supportive of, for, approving of

justification for

🌈： reason for, grounds for, basis for

🌈： cause of, basis for, grounds for

argue sb into doing sth

🌈： persuade sb to do sth, talk sb into doing sth, convince sb to do sth

argue sb out of doing sth

persuade sb to do sth

🌈： convince sb to do sth, talk sb into doing sth, coax sb into doing sth

talk sb into doing sth

🌈： persuade sb to do sth, convince sb to do sth, win sb over to doing sth

talk sb out of doing sth
💫： 说服某人不做某事

🌈： dissuade sb from doing sth, discourage sb from doing sth, advise sb against doing sth

🌈： resist, oppose, fight back against

🌈： disregard, ignore, set aside

💫： 督促/逼迫某人做某事
🫧： His parents pushed him to apply to top universities.
🌈： urge sb to do sth, pressure sb to do sth, compel sb to do sth

💫： 领先于；超越；提前处理
🫧： We need to innovate constantly to get ahead of our competitors.
🌈： overtake, outpace, surpass, stay ahead of

🌈： stay ahead of, anticipate, preempt
